Donate

Every gift helps
a child be heard.

Brave Voices is sustained by people who believe that silence protects abuse and that speaking up protects children. Your gift — of any size — funds the education, resources, and community that turn ordinary adults into safe ones.

Why give

What your donation does.

Brave Voices is a small, lean nonprofit. That means your dollar goes directly to the work — not to overhead, not to marketing, not to expense accounts. Here's where it lands:

  • $25 prints and distributes one batch of conversation-starter cards to a local school or faith community.
  • $100 sponsors a caregiver through an evidence-based prevention training.
  • $500 funds a community workshop in a neighborhood that has asked for one.
  • $1,000+ underwrites survivor story preservation — recorded, archived, and held with care.
  • Monthly giving of any amount keeps the doors open and the hotline-adjacent resources free for families who need them.
Give now

Make a secure donation.

All gifts are processed securely through our donation partner. Brave Voices is a registered 501(c)(3); your donation is tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

Other ways to give

Not every gift comes with a card number.

Monthly

Become a sustaining donor.

Recurring gifts of $5, $10, or $25 per month give Brave Voices the one thing nonprofits rarely have — predictability. Set it up once in the form above.

In honor / in memory

Give in someone's name.

Honor a survivor, a teacher, a parent — or the memory of a child. We'll send a gentle acknowledgment card to whoever you designate.

Employer match

Double your impact.

Many employers match charitable contributions dollar-for-dollar. Ask your HR department and send the forms to donate@bravevoices.org.

Stock, DAF, or legacy

Non-cash giving.

Stock transfers, donor-advised fund grants, and legacy gifts are welcome. Email donate@bravevoices.org and we'll walk you through the options.

By mail

Write a check.

Make checks payable to Brave Voices and mail to the address on file. Email us for the current mailing address so your gift reaches us.

Not ready to give?

Share this page.

Posting the Brave Voices web address to your neighborhood listserv or social feed costs nothing and widens the safe-adult net. That's its own kind of gift.

Transparency
