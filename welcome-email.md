# Welcome to Brave Voices

**Subject line options:**
- Welcome to Brave Voices — we're glad you're here
- You're in. Thank you for using your voice with us.
- A gentle hello from Brave Voices

**Preheader:** Courage over silence — here's what you can expect from us.

---

Hello, friend,

Thank you for joining the Brave Voices community. Whatever brought you to us — your own story, someone you love, or a quiet conviction that children deserve safer worlds — we're honored to have you here.

Our mission is simple, even when the work isn't: **speak even when your voice shakes.** We choose courage over silence to protect children, support safe relationships, and help prevent childhood sexual abuse.

We want this inbox to feel like a steady hand, not another weight. You can read at your own pace, skip what isn't for you today, and come back when you're ready.

**A few places to begin, only when it feels right:**

- **[Learn](https://bravevoices.org/learn)** — gentle, plain-language information about prevention and the patterns abuse often follows.
- **[Guide](https://bravevoices.org/guide)** — what to say when a child discloses, including our "I hear you. I believe you." script for the moments that matter most.
- **[Take Action](https://bravevoices.org/take-action)** — reporting pathways, ways to share your story if you choose to, and how to get involved in your community.

You won't hear from us often, and never with anything sensational. Expect occasional notes with practical tools, stories shared with consent, and ways to help when you have the capacity.

If at any point this feels like too much, you can step away — unsubscribe links are at the bottom of every email, and there's no hard feelings.

With care and courage,

**The Brave Voices team**

*If you or someone you know needs immediate support, the RAINN hotline is available 24/7 at 1-800-656-HOPE (4673).*
